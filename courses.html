Courses

So far studying at Wisconsin-Madison has presented me with a variety of classes that I really have enjoyed. Here are some courses that I feel like represent my interests and strengths, and that I have found the amost enjoyment doing:

Art 107: Introduction to Digital Forms

Introduction to the theory and practice of digital media, including raster graphics, video, 3d modeling and coding technologies.

Com Arts 155: Introduction to Digital Media Production

You will learn important skills in digital media production, including web design and programming, high-definition photography, high-definition video shooting/editing, digital audio production, and linear and non-linear storytelling.

Cnsr Sci 250: Retail Leadership Symposium

This course is an exploration of the retailing industry, consumer products industry, and many businesses related to and serving these industries.

Gen Bus 106: Foundational Skills for Business Analysis

Build fundamental skills and processes to develop a strong foundation in business analysis utilizing Excel.
